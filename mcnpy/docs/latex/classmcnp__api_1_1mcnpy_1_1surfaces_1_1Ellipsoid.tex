\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
An \mbox{\hyperlink{classmcnp__api_1_1mcnpy_1_1surfaces_1_1Ellipsoid}{Ellipsoid}} defined by a 2 points/vectors and a radius. 

By default, {\ttfamily rm} is the major radius and {\ttfamily v1} and {\ttfamily v2} are the coordinates of the first and second focii. If {\ttfamily rm \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<} 0} (meaning {\ttfamily rm} is now the minor radius), then {\ttfamily v1} is the coordinates of the ellipsoid\textquotesingle{}s center and {\ttfamily v2} is its major axis vector.
