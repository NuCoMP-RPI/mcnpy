Detailed Description

An Ellipsoid defined by a 2 points/vectors and a radius.

By default, rm is the major radius and v1 and v2 are the coordinates of the first and second focii. If rm < 0 (meaning rm is now the minor radius), then v1 is the coordinates of the ellipsoid's center and v2 is its major axis vector.
